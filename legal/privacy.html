Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Granny vs Robot ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use our Reddit mini-game provided via the Reddit Developer Platform (Devvit).

2. Data We Collect

When you interact with Granny vs Robot on Reddit, we may collect the following information provided by the Reddit API:

  • Reddit Username: We collect your Reddit username to display your score on the public leaderboard.
  • Gameplay Data: We track your highest score and maximum wave reached to rank you on the leaderboard.
  • Subscription Status: We track if you have opted-in (subscribed) to receive updates or notifications related to the game.

We do not collect passwords, personal email addresses, or any other sensitive personal data outside of what you publicly authorize via Reddit.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data strictly for the following purposes:

  • To operate and maintain the game's leaderboard.
  • To track your in-game progress (score and waves).
  • To send you game updates if you have explicitly subscribed to them.
  • To comply with Reddit's Developer Terms and improve overall gameplay experience.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Reddit's provided Redis infrastructure (Devvit Datastore). We apply industry-standard practices to protect your information, but please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your Reddit username and gameplay score will be publicly visible to other Reddit users via the in-game leaderboard. Your data is also subject to Reddit's own Privacy Policy.

6. Your Rights

Because your participation is tied to your Reddit account, you may stop using the game at any time to prevent further data collection. If you wish to have your leaderboard records removed, please contact the developer through Reddit.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via Reddit at the subreddit: r/granny_vs_robot_dev or through the user account that published this game.
